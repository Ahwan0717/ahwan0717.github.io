Hello Stranger,

My Name is Ariana Hwang

I'm 21 years old and live in Long Island. Most of my days are spent in the most outlandish and exciting city in the world— New York City. On Tuesdays and Thursdays, I take three classes at Baruch College: Business Policy, Narrative Writing, and Web Design. When June comes around and the spring semester is over, I'll be receiving my Bachelor’s Degree with a major in digital marketing and journalism. For now though, during my “free days”, I like to explore the city’s nightlife, finding more concert venues, clubs, bars, and restaurants to love. Thrift stores and museum galleries also call my attention. Besides exploring, you will probably find me working on my other website Lullavie, organizing concerts, and interviewing musicians on WBMB radio. Doing all of this has made me realize my desire to be a creative and serial entrepreneur someday.
